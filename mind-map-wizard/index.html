Define Your Problem

Start by clearly describing the problem or challenge you want to explore. A well-defined problem is the foundation of effective mind mapping.

Mind Mapping Principles

  • Start with a central idea or problem in the center
  • Use branches to connect related ideas and concepts
  • Keep branch labels concise (1-3 words ideal)
  • Use colors to categorize and differentiate themes
  • Build hierarchically from general to specific concepts

Example Problem Statements

Team Communication

How can we improve team communication in our remote work environment?

Website Performance

What's causing our website's low conversion rate?

Product Launch

How should we approach launching our new product in a competitive market?
